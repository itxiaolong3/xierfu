Princeton University最喜欢什么样的学生？

School achievement record and recommendations of guidance counselor and 2 teachers very important. SAT or ACT with Writing required along with 2 SAT Subject Test scores. For applicants pursuing BSE degree one of the SAT Subject Tests should be in either physics or chemistry; the other in mathematics (Level I or II).
